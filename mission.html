Mission

Manufacturing work shouldn’t live in inboxes. Order Pool exists to turn production briefs into trackable orders — with clear ownership, fewer misunderstandings, and less chasing.

How it works Get started

Principles

Safety & trust

Pull beats push

Manufacturers accept work when it matches their capacity. Publishers don’t have to chase quotes.

Standard briefs

One clear format reduces rework, speeds quoting, and anchors execution to a shared source of truth.

Transparent progress

Accepted orders move through explicit states from acceptance to fulfillment.

Updates syndicate

Manufacturer updates automatically appear on publisher dashboards.

Status-only public tracking

Visibility builds trust without exposing sensitive pricing or assets.

Less noise, more execution

The system is designed to reduce busywork and make delivery the default.

What we’re building

A shared request pool that treats manufacturing like a modern workflow: a paid brief becomes a quote request, a quote becomes an order, and the order becomes proof of execution.

Step 1

Brief

Publishers create standardized requests that are easy to evaluate.

Step 2

Pool

Paid briefs enter a shared quote pool visible to manufacturers.

Step 3

Order

Accepted quotes become orders with ownership and states.

Step 4

Proof

Status tracking creates accountability and trust.
