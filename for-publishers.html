Publisher flow

Draft → submit + pay → in pool → accepted → track
1
Create a brief
Define quantity, timeline, constraints, and attach assets while drafting privately.
2
Submit + pay
Publishing turns your brief into a paid quote request and places it in the pool.
3
Manufacturers review
Manufacturers browse the pool and accept work that fits their capacity and capability.
4
It becomes an order
Acceptance converts the request into an order you can track in your dashboard.
5
Updates syndicate
Manufacturer updates post once and syndicate automatically into your order detail view.
6
Stay aligned
Ask questions inside the order thread so decisions and context never disappear.
