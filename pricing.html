FAQ

What does a publisher pay for?

Publishing a brief into the pool. The fee converts the brief into a quote request visible to manufacturers.

Do manufacturers pay to browse?

Manufacturers can browse and accept work in the pool, then manage accepted orders and post updates.

What shows publicly?

Only accepted orders and their status. Pricing and private notes remain private.
