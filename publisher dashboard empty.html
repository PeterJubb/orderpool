Start here

A simple path to get your first request into the pool.

1) Create a brief

Define what you’re producing (quantity, timeline, constraints, sampling needs).

2) Submit + pay

Submitting and paying publishes your request to the manufacturer quote pool.

3) Get accepted

A manufacturer accepts the request. Your quote request becomes a trackable order.

4) Track progress

Manufacturer updates syndicate to this dashboard until the order is fulfilled.
